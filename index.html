Probability Language Scale

Before computing, ask students to predict roughly where an event sits on this scale — it catches answers that are numerically "correct" but conceptually impossible (like a probability of 1.4).

0
Impossible 0.25
Unlikely 0.5
Equally Likely 0.75
Likely 1
Certain

An event is typically flagged as unusual when its probability is 0.05 or less.
